    UX Overhaul

    The objective of the UX Overhaul assignment was to identify a flawed user experience (UX) design and enhance it. Students were given considerable freedom in selecting the design they wished to improve and the tools they preferred to utilize. Consequently, I chose to enhance the navigation bar of CNN's website. Engaging in this assignment provided a valuable opportunity to revisit and reinforce my knowledge before embarking on semester three.
