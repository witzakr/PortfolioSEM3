    Passionate Project: Empathy map

    Based on the data from the surveys and interviews, we created an empathy map to organize and understand our findings. The empathy map is divided into four sections: what users do, say, feel, and think. This format gave us valuable insights for designing the studio page, as it helps us collect, format, and display data gathered from our research in a clear and structured way.
