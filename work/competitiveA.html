    Group Project: Competitive Analysis

    Based on the research conducted, our group prepared two competitive analysis documents. One focused on interactive story games, while the other covered events in Eindhoven commemorating WW2 events in the area. Both analyses were structured using the SWOT method (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, and Threats).
    This approach provided valuable insights into the content and features we should incorporate into our final project.
