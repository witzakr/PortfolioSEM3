    Group Project: Empathy map

    Our team made an empathy map using the data we collected. It's divided into four parts: thinks, says, does, and feels. It focuses on how people research World War II, and what they think, feel, say, and do. I think this practice is important because it helps us understand our target audience's actions and behaviors better and connect with them more deeply.
