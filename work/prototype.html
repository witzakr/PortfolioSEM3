    Portfolio Prototype

    After creating a wireframe, it was time to create a prototype for my website. Firstly, I had to decide on the main colors, so I opted for light blue, black, and white to give the website a professional look. These colors also suited both me and my teachers, so it seemed like a good choice.
    Next, it was time to focus on styling. I placed significant emphasis on the buttons and animations on my website, so it was important that the style complemented them well. Additionally, I chose a thick font to ensure readability wouldn't be an issue.
    Finally, I put everything together, and I considered the result sufficient before moving on to creating my website.
