    Group Project: Paper Prototype

    Before preparing a full prototype, it was important to create a paper one to properly plan it out before starting the full version. We prepared some sketches beforehand, but they were just to ensure everyone in the group was on the same page regarding the website's layout and design. We then began working on the paper prototype and decided that the first section of the website would be for video display and the second one for written content.
    After that, one of our group members tested the prototype with our target audience. The feedback was to improve the footer so it doesn't look empty, center the navigation bar links, and add some motion to them. These improvements were made, and overall, the paper prototype was a success.
