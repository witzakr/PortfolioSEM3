    Group Project: Concept Document

    This document represents the second phase of our project, where we delve deeper into our concept. We introduce Points of View (POVs) to provide insights into our chosen target audience, including their backgrounds, needs, and frustrations. Additionally, we pose "How might we" questions to explore potential avenues for enhancing user experience. For instance, we contemplate ways to create immersive digital experiences and utilize social media platforms effectively.
    Moreover, we present an empathy map, although its development might seem premature without a functional prototype. This visual tool aids in understanding user interactions, despite its early stage.
