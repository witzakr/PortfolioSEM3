    Group Project: Research Document

    The research document was a collection of all the research we did for our group project. It included information about how we gathered data, like through interviews and surveys, and it also had details about the personas we created and an empathy map. Since I played a big role in making the personas and crafting interviews, I thought it was important to include this document to show all the work we did for the project. By including everything in one place, we wanted to make sure that anyone reading it could understand the full scope of our research and the insights we gained from it.
