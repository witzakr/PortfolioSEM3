    Group Project: Brandbook

    As part of our project's branding phase, we created a brand book. It covered important aspects like our story, brand values, logo, typography, colors, and tone of voice. While I contributed ideas to the group discussions, my main task was to work on the tone of voice section. We decided on a caregiver tone together. This assignment was crucial because it serves as a summary of our group and will be shown to potential clients, so it needed to represent us well.
