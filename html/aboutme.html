    About me

    Hey there! I'm Wit Zakrzewski, a 20-year-old student at Fontys University of Applied Sciences in Eindhoven. Currently, I'm diving into the world of ICT-media design, a fascinating blend of technology and creative media. My passion lies in bridging the gap between technology and art, aiming to craft innovative solutions in the ever-evolving field of ICT-media design. Outside of my academic pursuits, you'll often find me indulging in my love for drawing, particularly simple shading techniques. I'm also an avid reader, with a particular fondness for history books. Sports, ranging across various interests, hold a special place in my heart, with basketball taking the lead. Additionally, I have coding experience in Python, C#, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, adding a tech-savvy layer to my diverse interests. My main aim right now is to complete my current course. After that, I plan to pursue a master's degree in computer science. Ultimately, I aspire to become a professional Full-stack developer. So follow along with me as I venture into this captivating academic pursuit!

    Propaedeutic Certificate

    On March 13th, I received a propaedeutic certificate from Fontys University, which marks the completion of my first year there. It's a significant achievement for me as it's my first university diploma and an important step in my journey to becoming a full-stack developer.
    The ceremony was enjoyable and meaningful. We got to see some of the best work done by students over the past two semesters, learned about future plans, and received valuable advice. After the ceremony, we had a small party to celebrate everyone's accomplishments. It was a fun way to end the day and mark this milestone in our academic and professional lives.

    Choosing Specialization

    After a thorough thought process of choosing my specialization, I decided to opt for Smart Mobile. Why? Because I believe it aligns perfectly with my skills and passions. What helped me with making my choice was advice from colleagues from higher semesters and assessing what aligns with my skills and profile. After that, I employed the elimination method and ended up choosing that specialization.
    Overall, this moment marks a special milestone in my journey as a media design developer, and I can't wait to embark on it, improve my current skills, and meet like-minded individuals in the field.
